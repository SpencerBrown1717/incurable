Autonomous Laboratory Systems

A fully integrated wet-lab workcell designed for closed-loop experimentation, sample custody and machine-readable provenance.

01 · Liquid Handler

Eight-channel pipetting head, disposable tip racks, reagent cooling, barcode verification and sub-microliter motion simulation.

02 · Robotic Sample Arm

Six-axis transfer robot with adaptive gripper, collision-safe joint trajectories and plate handoff across every instrument.

03 · Refrigerated Centrifuge

Enclosed rotor, animated lid interlock, sealed buckets, active cooling and programmable acceleration profiles.

04 · Incubator Stack

Four independent culture chambers with CO₂, humidity, temperature and internal imaging status indicators.

05 · Multimode Plate Reader

Motorized tray, optical excitation head and continuous fluorescence / absorbance quality-control stream.

06 · Instrumented Bioreactor

Jacketed vessel, impeller drive, gas sparging, sensor manifold and real-time process-control visualization.

Static deployment · no build step · WebGL required
